Dress Code

Skiers must wear long sleeves and appropriate hard-wearing trousers (salopettes if you have them) because falling on the matting is painful on bare skin. They must also wear waterproof gloves or mittens, and must wear a helmet which Hillend do provide or they can wear their own. Socks should come up to at least mid shin level so that they sit above the top of the boots.

Branded Gear

Buckstone Ski Club branded hoodies and t-shirts are available to purchase at the beginning of each term. Information will be provided at that time.
